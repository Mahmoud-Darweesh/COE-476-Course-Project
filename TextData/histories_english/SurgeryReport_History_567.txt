Patient referred by a specialist with histologically confirmed, moderately differentiated squamous cell carcinoma of the soft palate. Mirror endoscopy revealed an exophytic, partially ulcerating mass in the area of the base of the uvula on the anterior palatal arch extending to the left side. Sonographically V.a. cN1 neck status. There is now an indication for panendoscopy and tumor resection for squamous cell carcinoma that has already been histologically confirmed externally and for PEG placement.
